PS5 released!

Posted 5 Jan 2021, By Youssof

I know that the PS5 was released Nov 12 but I never had the chance to speak about it. I think that it has a really cool and futurestic realease. iPhone 12

Posted 5 Jan 2021, By Youssof

The iPhone 12 has been released, lets talk about it. It has a new boxy design that they brought back from the old iPhone SE which I presonally like. It can now support 5G which is nice, but a major new feature is that it can now record 4K HDR, and has a new 5nm processing chip (A14 Bionic). It came out in 4 different colors. But with such features comes a great price tag, the cheapest being the iPhone mini for $999! thats the cheapest!
